About Us

Who We Are

Kruthi Enterprises is a registered steel and metal trading company based in Davanagere, Karnataka. We source and supply high-quality steel products to businesses across industries, ensuring reliable delivery and competitive pricing.

  • ✓ We are authorised traders — not manufacturers — dealing in a wide range of certified steel and metal products from trusted sources.
  • ✓ Registered under GST and MSME, ensuring compliance and trustworthy business practices.
  • ✓ We are an export-ready enterprise, committed to expanding our reach to global markets in the near future.
  • ✓ Serving construction, infrastructure, engineering, and manufacturing sectors with quality-assured GI pipes, alloy bars, tubes, and flat products.
